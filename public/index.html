Space Flight

Multiple companies around the globe are racing to place the first man on Mars. The most notable exception includes Spacex, which aims to launch as soon as 2024, with its Techologically advanced SpaceCraft 'Starship'.

Conquer Size With Quantity

The new Horizons Telescope is an international collaboration with the aim of taking the first ever picture of a black hole. However, in order to capture enough light for this image, NASA would require a Telescope the size of Planet Earth! This barrier is overcome by having a total of 12 very large Telescopes positioned at various locations around the globe, all operating in synchrony.

Beyond our solar system:

There exist many strange phenomena outside of our immediate solar system which many researchers are still unable to fully understand. Amomg others, examples include black holes and the nature of dark matter. Although black holes have been physically observed, the presence of dark matter is still a theoretical notion.

FUTURE VENTURES:

Starship: Spacex mission to Mars

Spacex has begun construction and testing of the SpaceCraft that will take man to Mars. There have been various changes to design and lauch schedule, but the goal of travelling to Mars remains the same.

Exoplanet: Proxmia Centuri B

Proxima Centuri b is an Earth-like planet (exoplanet) which orbits around a Star known as Proxmia Centuri. This orbit is at a distance which is thought to allow habitation on the planets surface
